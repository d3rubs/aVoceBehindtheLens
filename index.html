About Me


 Hi.
  I'm Jen Voce.
I enjoy sushi, beaches, Richard Dean Anderson and baseball. (aka, totally wholesome)
I also love everything photography.

The name “Voce” pronounced "VO-CHEE" means "voice".
My photography is my voice. It shouts the love of newlyweds, the whispering awe of my family, the call of my competitive sport, and warm conversations between old friends.

Let me be your voice behind the lens!
